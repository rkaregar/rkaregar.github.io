Reza Karegar

I am a Software Engineer at Google, working on Vertex AI Search and Agent Builer. Before Vertex AI, I was a member of the Bigtable team, where I was responsible for developing an Apache Spark connector for Bigtable, along with other client-side features.
Prior to joining Google, I was a Computer Science Master's student at the University of Waterloo, advised by Prof. Lukasz Golab. My research focused on discovering data constraints in relational databases. I finished my undergraduate studies at Sharif University of Technology, studying Computer Engineering.
